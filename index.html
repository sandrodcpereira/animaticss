Why use frame animations on the web?

What are frame animations?

Frame animations are composed of individual pictures that, when played in sequence, give the illusion of movement.

For Animaticss, animations frames are precomposed into a horizontal strip — like film in an old movie.

Why use frame animations?

Here's some reasons to use this method:

  • It can be achieved with simple HTML and CSS. Zero dependencies.

  • You have control over when the animation plays. Trigger it when specific events occur, like on hover or a button press.

  • Only requires one asset per animation. That’s a lot easier than handling multiple assets.

  • With more code, one asset can provide multiple animations!

Not a bad option for when you’re looking to add some quick and lightweight animations to your projects.
